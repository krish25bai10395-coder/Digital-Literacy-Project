Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
🔐 General Safety Tips
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone.
Do not click suspicious links sent via SMS or WhatsApp.
Always verify unknown callers claiming to be bank officials.
Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts.
Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms.
Install apps only from official sources like Google Play Store.
💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips (Compulsory)
Remember: You do NOT need to enter a UPI PIN to receive money.
Never scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
Check transaction details carefully before approving payments.
